# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Self-hosted AI gateway operators and end users who chat with multiple upstream models (OpenAI, Claude, Gemini, DeepSeek, etc.) in a single interface. They switch models and capabilities mid-flow and expect controls that feel native, dense, and trustworthy—not like a vendor clone.

## Product Purpose

Prism is a unified, self-hosted AI gateway and chat platform. Success means users can configure model behavior (thinking depth, tools, search) quickly while chatting, with clear state and minimal friction.

## Brand Personality

Calm, precise, restrained. Interface should disappear into the task; confidence comes from clarity and consistency, not decoration.

## Anti-references

- Claude/OpenAI/Gemini official UI clones (especially the Faster↔Smarter effort slider)
- Purple-glow “AI aesthetic” accents as default branding
- Over-animated or novelty controls for standard settings

## Design Principles

1. **Earned familiarity** — Prefer patterns users already trust in product tools (lists, toggles, segmented controls).
2. **State over spectacle** — Selection, enabled/disabled, and depth must be obvious at a glance.
3. **One vocabulary** — Shared controls across model-specific thinking popovers.
4. **Density with breath** — Compact chat chrome; popovers stay scannable, not sparse or cluttered.
5. **Own the interaction** — Avoid looking like a pasted vendor widget.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and controls. Honor `prefers-reduced-motion` (instant or crossfade instead of choreography). Keyboard operable for thinking-level selection (arrows, Home/End, Enter/Space).
